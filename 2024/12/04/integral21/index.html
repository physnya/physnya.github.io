/Theorem/

Rn+1=f(n+1)(ξ)n!(x−ξ)n(x−0)R_{n+1}=\frac{f^{(n+1)}(\xi)}{n!}(x-\xi)^n(x-0)Rn+1​=n!f(n+1)(ξ)​(x−ξ)n(x−0)
